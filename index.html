The Scribble
Society

We often treat young people as incapable of deep, introspective thought. But instead of completely and weighing them down with an expectation of greatness, it is important for children to access spaces where they can cultivate their artistic passion alongside peers and independent of censorship. The Scribble Society is a forum where young creatives can publish their work and view works made by peers who share the same passions. Submissions are posted in a monthly zine.
